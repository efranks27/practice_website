Who We Are

Peak is a platform that allows gamers to improve their skills and learn new strategies through a variety of resources, such as video tutorials, live coaching sessions, and forums for discussion and collaboration.
We cater to a a number of games and genres (always expanding), and provide a community for players to connect and learn from one another.

We offer a variety of features such as live streaming of gameplay, personalized training sessions, and progress tracking to help users keep track of their progress and set goals.
We also have a number of professional players who act as mentors and provide one-on-one coaching sessions. Peak also offers a looking for team(lft) feature that allows users to find and play with other players of similar skill level.
Overall, Peak is a valuable resource for gamers looking to improve their skills and become more competitive in their favorite games.

Featured Games:

  • Rocket League

    • Rocket League is a high-powered hybrid of arcade-style soccer and vehicular mayhem with easy-to-understand controls and fluid, physics-driven competition. Players are equipped with booster-rigged vehicles that can be crashed into balls for incredible goals or epic saves across multiple, highly-detailed arenas. The game includes single player and multiplayer modes and is available on a variety of platforms including PC, Xbox, PlayStation and Nintendo Switch. It has been well-received by both critics and players for its unique gameplay and has a dedicated esports scene
    The game also features a Battle Pass system, where players can purchase a season-long pass and earn rewards by completing challenges and leveling up.

  • Fortnite

    • Fortnite is a free-to-play battle royale game developed and published by Epic Games. The game is available on a variety of platforms including PC, Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch, and mobile devices. In the game, up to 100 players parachute onto an island and scavenge for weapons and resources while fighting to be the last person or team standing. The game also features a creative mode where players can build structures and create their own worlds.
    Fortnite is known for its unique and colorful art style, as well as its constantly evolving map and regular updates. The game has a large and dedicated player base, and has become popular in the esports scene. Additionally, Fortnite has become well-known for its collaborations with other popular media properties, such as Marvel, Star Wars, and the NFL.
    The game also features a Battle Pass system, where players can purchase a season-long pass and earn rewards by completing challenges and leveling up.
    Fortnite has become one of the most popular video games in recent years, with a massive player base and a large cultural impact.

  • Overwatch 2

    • Overwatch 2 is a first-person shooter game developed by Blizzard Entertainment. It is the sequel to the highly popular and successful game, Overwatch. The game features new maps, new heroes, and new game modes. The game also introduces new PvE (player versus environment) content, in which players work together to complete a series of cooperative missions against AI-controlled enemies. The game also features a new "Push" mode, a new objective-based game mode that has teams competing to capture a point and push it to the enemy's side of the map.
    Overwatch 2 also features updated graphics and new character designs. The game also introduces new abilities and ultimates for existing heroes. It also features cross-play, allowing players on different platforms to play together.

  • Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege

    • Rainbow Six Siege is a first-person shooter game developed and published by Ubisoft. The game is focused on tactical, team-based gameplay and features a variety of operators, each with their own unique gadgets and abilities. The game takes place in various locations around the world and features a variety of game modes, including a 5v5 mode called "Secure Area" where teams must work together to either defend or attack an objective.
    Rainbow Six Siege is known for its destructible environments and intense, adrenaline-pumping action. The game features a variety of operators, each with their own unique gadgets and abilities, which players can use to gain an advantage in combat. The game also has a strong emphasis on teamwork and communication, making it a popular choice for players who enjoy a more tactical, strategic experience.
    Rainbow Six Siege has a strong esports scene, with regular tournaments and a dedicated community of professional and semi-professional players. The game also receives regular updates and new content, including new operators, maps, and game modes.
    Rainbow Six Siege was released in December 1st, 2015 and still receives updates and new content, including new operators, maps, and game modes
